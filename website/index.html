Graff

Want to see how your courses all fit together? Want to visualise how your courses lead to one another? Want to know which courses you still need to take to take the courses you want to take?

Graff (pronounced Grass) helps you visualise all the courses you have taken, are taking and want to take and the relationships between them.

We present a intuitive, graphical representation of what your courses are going to be, making it much MUCH easier for you plan your future. No more fussing about Schedule of Classes and SIO for ugly dependency relations!
